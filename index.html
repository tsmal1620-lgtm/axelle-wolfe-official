Axelle Wolfe is a rising powerhouse of the Quebec drag scene, known for high-octane performances and a polished, avant-garde aesthetic.

A fierce competitor with a reputation for precision, she was awarded Newcomer of the Year at the 2023 Gala Des Artistes. Based in Québec City, Axelle performs across Ontario and Québec.
