AXELLE WOLFE

BIO BIO

Axelle Wolfe is a rising powerhouse of the Quebec drag scene, known for high-octane performances and a polished, avant-garde aesthetic. Since exploding onto the stage in 2022, Axelle has quickly established herself as a consistent "one-to-watch."

A fierce competitor with a reputation for precision, she was awarded Relève de l'Année at the 2023 Gala Des Artistes after a stunning second-place finish in Dragwarts at Le Drague in Québec City. Her momentum continued across borders, tying for second in Guelph’s Next Drag Superstar (2025) and most recently claiming second place in the high-stakes Drague On (2026) at Le Drague Cabaret.
